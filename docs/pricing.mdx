Mecha Pay is designed to be a low-cost, high-performance protocol. Our fee structure is simple and transparent, ensuring that merchants keep the vast majority of their revenue.

## Protocol Fees

The protocol charges a flat percentage fee on every subscription transaction. This fee is used to maintain the decentralized indexers and support the protocol's development.

| Category | Fee | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Protocol Fee** | **2.5%** | Applied to every subscription payment. |
| **Listing Fee** | **Free** | Create unlimited plans and tiers at no cost. |
| **Management** | **Free** | Revoke, update, or manage your plans for free. |

## How it works

When a user pays for a subscription, the smart contract automatically splits the payment:

1. **Merchant Share (97.5%)**: Instantly transferred to your merchant wallet.
2. **Protocol Share (2.5%)**: Transferred to the protocol treasury.

### Example

If a user subscribes to a **$100.00** plan:
- You receive: **$97.50**
- Protocol receives: **$2.50**

## Gas Fees

Since Mecha Pay runs on high-performance EVM networks (like Arc, Base, etc.), gas fees are negligible, typically costing less than **$0.01** per transaction.

## Settlement

All payments are settled instantly on-chain. There are no holding periods or payouts; your revenue is available in your wallet the moment a user completes their checkout.
